Lightning Talks

Lightning Talks are 2-minute presentations by students about a topic of their choosing.

Sign up for your spot here.

Why Are We Doing This?

Being able to speak LOUDLY, CLEARLY, and CONFIDENTLY in front of an audience is a great life skill. As a software engineer, you need to be able to express your opinions and thoughts to your team. How should the architecture look like? What frameworks should you use? Why is this particular paradigm so important?

Being able to express your views, tell a story, or teach is a skill all leaders need to have.

What Do I Do?

RBK Lightning Talks will happen after lunch every day starting tomorrow.

They must be 2-minutes

You can talk about any topic that you would like. But in our experience, non-technical lectures are always the best.

Do I Have to Do This? I Am Terrible at Public Speaking!!

Yes. This is mandatory. We will make sure everyone goes at least once. This is your chance to practice public speaking in a safe environment! Practice, practice, practice.

Do I Need to Create a PowerPoint Presentation?

No.

I Don't Know What To Talk About

Talk about something that matters to you. What do you care about? What do you talk about with your friends and family? What captures your attention when you turn on the TV? Corgis in Costumes?

Tips

  • Speak loudly.
  • If you act confident, you will feel confident!
  • Make sure this is a topic that is meaningful for you.
  • Please respect all talks given.
